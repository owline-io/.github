## What is Owline?

Owline is a **social network where playlists are posts**. Users curate playlists, publish them to their feed, and the community engages through loves, saves, comments, and forks.

---

## Features

- **Playlist-as-Post** — curate and publish playlists as social posts
- **Feed & Discover** — follow users, trending playlists, mood filters
- **Scrobbling** — automatic music tracking from any player
- **Music Imports** — bring your listening history from other platforms
- **Social** — follow, love, save, comment, fork playlists, direct messages
- **Search** — find users, playlists, artists, and tracks
